A Novel Hybrid Resampling Algorithm for Parallel/Distributed Particle Filters and Its Convergence Analysis.
Xudong Zhang , Liang Zhao, Wei Zhong, Feng Gu
Journal of Parallel and Distributed Computing (IPDC) 2020

This is the first time the parallel Monte Carlo method is mathematically proved to be uniformly convergent since it was introduced in 2000. The proof provided a solid theoretical foundation for its application in computer vision, object tracking, modeling, and simulation.
